About the project

Documentation & digital preservation of Ethiopia's languages

The Ethio Language Box Project is dedicated to the documentation and digital preservation of Ethiopian languages — creating multilingual self-study guidebooks, mobile apps, online courses, and audio pronunciation tools to make language learning accessible to everyone.

We believe that preserving languages is essential to maintaining cultural heritage and diversity.
